Frequently asked questions

Do you replace employee judgment?

No. AI speeds up work, improves consistency, and helps with first drafts. Staff still own final business decisions.

How are requests submitted?

Simple form, email, or shared portal. The intake process can be standardized for the client after onboarding.

What counts as one request?

One business question, task, or use case. Minor clarifications stay inside the original request.

What kind of businesses is this for?

Small businesses that want practical AI help without hiring an internal specialist or wasting time on generic training.
