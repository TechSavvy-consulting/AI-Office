FAQ

Common questions

Do you replace employee judgment?

No. The goal is to speed up work, improve consistency, and reduce blank-page time. Staff still own the final business decision.

How are requests submitted?

By form, email, or another simple intake method. The included SOP pack has a recommended workflow.

What counts as one request?

One business question, task, or use case submitted for a clear answer, draft, or workflow recommendation.

Can you train a whole office?

Yes. The free workshop is meant to introduce the service and identify the first practical use cases.
